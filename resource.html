Resources

College Board

College Board is a organization that connects students to college success and opportunities. They offer college-level classes for a fee, that can broaden students' intellectual horizons and prepare them for college work. They help students prepare for a successful transition to college through programs and services that specialize in college readiness and success. This includes the SAT and Advanced Placement Programs.

Bottom Line

Bottom Line is a non-profit ogranization that has assisted many low-income and first-generation students get into and stay in college, as well as earn their degrees. They assist students by mentoring and guiding them throughout the college application process and all through college in order to succeed

Khan Academy

Khan Academy is non-profit educational organization that offers online courses, lessons and practices excercises. They offer a personalized learning dashboard that enables students to study at their own pace. This personalized dashboard is also available for their SAT test prep activities. This organization provides help in math, science, computer programming, history, art history, economics, and more.

Khan Academy: Click here

Kaplan

Kaplan is a for-profit corporation that offers educational services. Their goal is to assist in thier educational endeavors. Kaplan prepares students for the SAT in order for them to, get into the college or university they aspire to be in and fulfill their desired career path through test prep.

Kaplan: Click here

A-List

A-List is an educational service provider that formalizes their unique and personal approach to education through their tutoring, school and non-profit programs. They assist students from all backgrounds in order for them to, accomplish their academic goals and fully prepare them for their future. A-List also offers SAT/ACT services to high school students.
