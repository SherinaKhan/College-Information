Freshmen

Advice for 9th Graders

Freshmen Orientation: When you attend the orientation, you will receive vital information pertaining to school life and procedures at Hillcrest.

Attendance: It is crucial to attend class. You need the required amount of credits in order to graduate. Credits can only be acquired by passing your classes. In order to pass you need to show up to class!

Extra-Curricular Activities: Being involved in different activities such as clubs, sports, college bound activies, etc. is a great experience. It is an amazing opportunity to meet upper-classmen in the school who share similar interests as you! Not only that but, being involved in certain activies may look good during the college application process.

PSAT: A practice exam that is taken during sophomore year. You can earn scholarship money towards college. It is never to early to start preparing for it!

Regents: These exams play a vital role in the graduation process and college admission. You need 5 passed regents to graduate. However, to prevent wasting your time and money on remedial classes in college, make sure you are college ready.
75 ELA & 80 Math Regents = College Ready

AP Courses: Start taking AP classes from sophomore year! These courses contain college level curriculum. Based off of your AP exam score, you could gain you FREE college credits.
